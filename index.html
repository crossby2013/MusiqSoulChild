LIVE LAUGH LOVE TOUR

Musiq Soulchild is coming to Cleveland, Ohio for his Live, Laugh, Love tour. The concert will be held at The House of Blues on December 18, 2015. Musiq has had numerous hits from his albums Aijuswanaseing, Juslisen, SoulStar, LuvaMusiq, On My Radio, and MusiqintheMagiq. Come reminisce for ONE NIGHT ONLY with Musiq's top hits such as Love, Teachme, DontChange, So Beautiful, Halfcrazy, Yes and B.U.D.D.Y.
